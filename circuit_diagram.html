⚠️ Important — Voltage Divider on D13 → ESP8266 D7:
Arduino Uno outputs 5V logic on TX. ESP8266 RX accepts max 3.3V. Use a resistor divider to protect the ESP:

Uno D13 ──[1kΩ]──┬── ESP D7 (RX)
                │
             [2kΩ]
                │
               GND

ESP8266 TX → Uno D12 is safe as direct connection (3.3V into 5V tolerant pin).
